Mach 1 Exterior

Ford extended the front and rear overhangs to make the Mustang bigger by 3.8 inches. Thus the wheelbase staying put at 108 inches. The roofline was also lowered by almost 1 inch.

Mach 1 Interior

High-back seats, teakwood-grained interior door panels, two-tone vinyl upholstery are all present and accounted for. With 3 circular holes drilled into each of its three spokes, the 3-spoke rim-blow steering wheel is as big as you'd expect.

Mach 1 Drivetrain

The top of the line engine was the Cobra Jet 7.0-liter V8 which you could option with a Ramair intake. That four-barrel mammoth produced 335 horsepower and 440 pound-feet of torque.
